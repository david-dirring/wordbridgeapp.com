Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a Family group?

Go to the Family tab, tap "Create Family," and give your group a name. You can then invite members by sharing an invite link or sending it directly via email or text.

How do I invite family members?

Open your Family group, tap "Invite Member," choose a role (Grandparent or Kid), and share the invite link. The recipient will need to install WordBridge and tap the link to join.

How does the kid scoring bonus work?

When a child's profile is set up in a Family group, their scores automatically get a multiplier based on their age: 3x for ages 5-7, 2x for ages 8-10, and 1.5x for ages 11-13. Parents can adjust or disable this in the child's settings.

Can my child play with strangers?

No. Children can only play with verified members of their Family groups. There is no public matchmaking. All Family members must be invited and approved by a Coordinator (parent).

How do I start a game against the computer?

Tap the + button on the Home screen, choose a difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard), and tap Start. You can play against the bot anytime, even without an internet connection.

What do the bonus squares mean?

The game board has four types of bonus squares: 2L (double letter value), 3L (triple letter value), 2W (double word score), and 3W (triple word score). Place high-value letters on these squares to maximize your score.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at david@pentupinnovation.com. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

Can I play on multiple devices?

Yes. Your games and family data sync across devices when you sign in with the same account. Start a game on your iPhone and continue on your iPad.

Is WordBridge safe for kids?

Yes. WordBridge is a private, family-only space with no public features, no ads, and no addictive mechanics. Kids can only play with verified family members. Parents maintain full visibility of all activity through the Activity Dashboard.

I forgot my password

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot Password" to receive a reset link via email. If you signed in with Apple, use your Apple ID to sign in again. For further help, contact us at david@pentupinnovation.com.

The app isn't loading / I'm experiencing a bug

Try these steps: (1) Close and reopen the app. (2) Check your internet connection. (3) Update to the latest version from the App Store. If the issue persists, email us at david@pentupinnovation.com with a description of the problem and your device model.
