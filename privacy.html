Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 7, 2026

WordBridge ("we", "our", or "the app") is a family word game developed by Pentup Innovation. WordBridge is a private, family-only space with no public features, no ads, and no addictive mechanics. We take your privacy seriously, especially because our app is designed for family use, including children.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Display name — chosen by you, shown to family members
  • Email address — used for authentication and account recovery
  • Authentication credentials — managed securely through Firebase Authentication (Apple Sign-In or email/password)

Game Data

During gameplay, we collect:

  • Game scores, moves, and match history
  • Personal statistics (wins, losses, average score, longest word, highest score)
  • In-game chat messages between family members

Family Data

When you create or join a Family group:

  • Family group name and membership
  • Member roles (Coordinator, Grandparent, Kid)
  • Age information for children (used for scoring handicap adjustments)
  • Family activity feed events

Device Information

To enable turn notifications, we may collect:

  • Push notification token (used solely to deliver "it's your turn" notifications)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device contacts
  • We do not use advertising trackers or analytics SDKs
  • We do not sell any user data to third parties

2. How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used solely for app functionality:

  • Authenticating your account and managing sign-in
  • Enabling multiplayer games between family members
  • Displaying game scores, stats, and history
  • Delivering in-game chat messages to other players
  • Managing Family groups and member roles
  • Applying age-based scoring adjustments for children

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the app's core functionality.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

WordBridge is designed for family use, including children under 13. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • Parental control: Children's accounts are created and managed by a parent or guardian within a Family group. A Coordinator (parent) must invite and approve all members.
  • No stranger contact: Children can only interact with verified Family group members. There is no public matchmaking or contact with strangers.
  • Limited data collection: We collect only the minimum data needed for gameplay (display name, age for scoring, game data).
  • Chat oversight: Parents can view all chat activity through the Activity Dashboard.
  • No behavioral advertising: We do not serve ads or use any behavioral tracking for children or any users.
  • Parental access and deletion: Parents can request to view or delete their child's data at any time by contacting us.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in (passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text)
  • Cloud Firestore for game data, family groups, and chat messages
  • All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firestore security rules restrict data access to authorized users only
  • Your games and family data sync across devices when using the same account

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud: Our infrastructure provider, which processes data on our behalf under their privacy policy
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data (edit your display name in Settings)
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data upon request

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at david@pentupinnovation.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. The "Effective date" at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
